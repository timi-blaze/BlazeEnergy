About the job software Engineer

Location:
This is an in-person role based in Port-harcourt our Rivers state office. However, we welcome applications from candidates residing outside of rivers state who are willing to work remotely.

Objective:
We are seeking an experienced, well rounded field intern to join our Engineering team in Nigeria. We need people that enjoy learning new technologies and tackling big chanllenges. You will buildd tools that Blaze uses to manage pan-African fleet of distributed energy systems. Our products include mobile and web applicatons that manage customers, energy production, assets, and transactions. You will help build new functionality and products over existing structures and help the Engineering team build new tools from scratch.

This is an opportunity to join as an early menmber of the Engineering team at Blaze, where you will be able to grow with a team around you to build solutions for some of the greatest challenges facing the continent and the world: climate change and energy access.

Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to the development of web and mobile applications
  • Design scalable data models and build well structured databases
  • Solve complex performance problems and architectural challenges
  • Evaluate new technologies to help evolve our technology stack

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Startup experience (high aptitude learner, small teams, multiple hats, prioritization)
  • Experienced in building great software tools from design to deployment
  • Effective communication, ability to learn quickly and deliver high quality code in a fast-paced, dynamic team environment
  • Knowledge and experience building with modern web development technologies and web based protocols
  • Experience developing mobile applications with kotlin using Andriod Studio.
  • Experience with PHP web framework (laravel), postgres databases, and linux servers.
  • Experience with cloud deployment

Tools that our Engineers currently work with:

  • particle Electron IoT plartform, STM32 And ATmega328PB microcontrollers
  • C++ for firmware and CircuitStudio PCBA for ECAD
  • Work in PHP/Laravel, Andriod Studio for software development
  • Work in python, SQL, Postgres for data science

Other things:

  • We are not tied down to current architectures, practices, or languages
