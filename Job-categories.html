About the job Field Tech Intern

Company Overview

Blaze is a solar energy start-up working to power Sub-Saharan Africa. We build solar-powered shops which charge batteries customers use to power their home. Since 2022, we've acheived over 3 million battery swaps, providing approximately 225,000 people access to clean, affordable energy.

Job Objective:
We're offering a six-month internship for aspiring and passionate field technicians with posibility of transitioning into full-time position. This role is based in Osogbo osun state.

Role and responsibilities:

As a field technician intern, you will:

  • Test, troubleshoot and repair defective products (Lithium -ion batteries and printed circuit board assemblies)
  • Design scalable data models and build well structured databases
  • Solve complex performance problems and architectural challenges
  • Evaluate new technologies to help evolve our technology stack

Requirements:

  • A diploma or Bachelor's degree in electrical Engineering, electronics, mechatronics, renewable energy from any recognized institution.
  • 1-2 years experience as a field technician
  • Effective communication, ability to learn quickly and deliver high quality code in a fast-paced, dynamic team environment
  • Knowledge and experience building with modern web development technologies and web based protocols
  • Experience developing mobile applications with kotlin using Andriod Studio.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Experience with cloud deployment

Tools that our Engineers currently work with:

  • particle Electron IoT plartform, STM32 And ATmega328PB microcontrollers
  • C++ for firmware and CircuitStudio PCBA for ECAD
  • Work in PHP/Laravel, Andriod Studio for software development
  • Work in python, SQL, Postgres for data science

Why join us:

  • We value well written documentation
  • We are not tied down to current architectures, practices, or languages
  • We are naturally curious and frequently exploring new technologies
